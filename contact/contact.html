Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a reservation?

You can make a reservation by searching for your preferred restaurant on our website, selecting the date, time, and party size, and then confirming your details. Alternatively, you can contact our customer service team for assistance.

Can I modify or cancel my reservation?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your reservation up to 24 hours before your scheduled dining time through your account dashboard. For last-minute changes, please contact the restaurant directly.

Is there a fee for using PrimeSeats?

PrimeSeats is completely free for diners. We never charge fees for making reservations through our platform. Some restaurants may have their own cancellation policies or require deposits for certain bookings.
