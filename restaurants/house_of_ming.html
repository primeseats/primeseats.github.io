House of Ming

House of Ming

54 Buckingham Gate St. James’ Court London, A Taj Hotel, London SW1E 6AF England

Chinese, Seafood

££££

Website

+44 20 7963 8330

House of Ming comes highly recommended by diners for its inviting atmosphere and diverse selection of delectable Chinese dishes, with the Peking Duck receiving particular praise. While most guests applaud the attentive service, a few have noted occasional delays.


Advanced bookings are advised due to its popularity, and while some report inconsistencies with food quality, the overall value is appreciated for the exceptional service and menu variety. However, one might find the music selection occasionally out of sync with the cultural vibe.
